¿Dónde Está la Pupusa?

A Salvadoran Memory Game

How to Play

Click on a card to flip it. Try to find matching pairs of Salvadoran icons. If the cards match, they will stay face up. If they do not match, they will flip back down. Match all pairs to win.

Haz clic en una carta para empezar.
