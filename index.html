How to Play

Welcome to this little Salvi-style memory game! You’ll find 24 cards inspired by our cultura — from pupusas and atol shuco to the torogoz and maquilishuat.

Your mission is simple: find all 12 matching pairs hidden on the board.

Flip two cards at a time:

If they match → ¡Eso cipote/cipota, good job!
If not → púchica… almost, try again.

No stress here — just vibes and fun. Every flip helps you remember where everything is hiding.

When you’re ready, hit Play Again. The timer (coming soon) is just for fun, so play at your own pace.

Haz clic en una carta para empezar.
